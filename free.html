Free Software

All of the software that I create is free software available under the GNU General Public License.

The word "free" used here means freedom, it isn't about price (though my programs do happen to be available at no cost). Freedom here means that you have the right to run, copy, study, change, and redistribute my programs, provided that you extend that same freedom to others.

For the exact legal terms and conditions, see the text of the GNU GPL.

About Me

I am a hobbyist programmer with an interest in game development. Until recently, I mostly worked with C++, but I am now learning Rust. I also have some experience with Java, Python, and Ruby. For web development, I am comfortable with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP.

All of the software I develop is free as in freedom. This means that you are free to run, copy, study, change, and redistribute my programs for any purpose provided that you extend those same freedoms to others, under the terms specified by the program's license.

Aside from coding, I also have a fondness for mathematics. In my free time I often read and learn about the subject. I came close to finishing a bachelor's degree in mathematics at the University of Maryland, but I left school due to a health problem.

You may contact me at gkikola@gmail.com. Feel free to ask questions or send bug reports. I also like hearing feedback, positive or negative, about my software projects, so please send your comments if you feel so inclined.

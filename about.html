About Me

I am a hobbyist programmer with an interest in game development. While I mostly work with C++, I also have experience with Java, Python, and Ruby. Regarding web development, I am comfortable with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP.

I am currently an undergraduate student at the University of Maryland, majoring in mathematics. I expect to graduate in Fall 2019. Prior to my time at UMD, I graduated from the Community College of Baltimore County with a 4.0 GPA and various other distinctions.

Aside from coding, I enjoy games: I play a good variety of board games and video games. With board games I tend to prefer light- to medium-weight eurogames and I am particularly fond of the work of Stefan Feld. For video games I enjoy a wide variety of genres, but I especially like puzzle games, RPGs, and platformers.

You may contact me at gkikola@gmail.com. Feel free to ask questions or send bug reports. I also like hearing feedback, positive or negative, about my software projects, so please send your comments if you feel so inclined.

About Me

I am a lifelong student of mathematics and a hobbyist programmer with an interest in game development.

Due to some health concerns, I am on a temporary break from school, but I plan to return in Spring 2020 to finish my bachelor's degree in mathematics.

All of the software I develop is free as in freedom. This means that you are free to run, copy, study, change, and redistribute my programs for any purpose provided that you extend those same freedoms to others, under the terms specified by the program's license.

My unofficial mathematics textbook solution guides are also completely free.

You may contact me at gkikola@gmail.com. Feel free to ask questions, to send bug reports for my software, or to send error reports for my textbook solutions.

About Me

I am a hobbyist programmer with an interest in game development. While I mostly work with C++, I also have experience with Java, Python, and Ruby. For web development, I am comfortable with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP.

I am currently an undergraduate student at the University of Maryland, majoring in mathematics. I expect to graduate in Fall 2019. Prior to my time at UMD, I graduated from the Community College of Baltimore County with a 4.0 GPA and various other distinctions.

Aside from coding, I enjoy games: I play a good variety of board games—particularly light- to medium-weight euros—and video games of various genres—especially puzzle games, RPGs, and platformers.

You may contact me at gkikola@gmail.com. Feel free to ask questions or send bug reports. I also like hearing feedback, positive or negative, about my software projects, so please send your comments if you feel so inclined.
